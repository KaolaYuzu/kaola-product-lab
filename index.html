Kaola Product Lab · Creator

About the Creator

Who I Am

I am an AI product builder. I turn scattered AI ideas, creative workflows, testing notes, and handoff documents into product systems that can be used, validated, and improved.

What I Am Building

I am building Kaola Product Lab — a product lab for testing AI web apps, document reconstruction tools, music release workflows, QA systems, and creator workflows.

Each island on this map represents a real project. Some are already available to try, some are being documented as case notes, and some represent capability systems I am still building.

Core Skills
AI Workflow Design Product QA Prompt / Spec / Handoff Writing Document System Human-in-the-loop Launch Readiness
Collaboration & Conversation

If you have an idea you want to turn into a website, a web tool, or a portfolio page, or if there is a workflow that feels stuck and you are wondering whether AI or web tools could help, feel free to reach out.

I am currently learning what creators, small brands, and freelancers actually struggle with when organizing content, presenting work, building websites, creating workflow tools, or applying AI in practical ways.

If the direction feels right, we can start with a small website, a template, an interactive tool, or a workflow prototype. If it is still unclear, we can first clarify the problem together.

Kaola Product Lab · Core Skills

Core Skills Overview

My work is not only about using individual AI tools. It is about connecting AI tools, product workflows, QA, documentation, and iteration records into practical systems that can be used and improved.

Skill Modules
1. AI Workflow Design

Turning AI tools into repeatable workflows, including input structure, decision points, output format, human review, and iteration.

2. Product QA

Checking whether a product actually works for users by reviewing flows, interface states, edge cases, and delivery quality.

3. Prompt / Spec / Handoff Writing

Turning rough ideas into prompts, product specs, revision instructions, handoff documents, and QA checklists that builders can follow.

4. Document System

Building documentation layers such as README, product specs, DESIGN, QA, HANDOFF, and PDCA so projects can be tracked, handed off, and reused.

5. Human-in-the-loop

Keeping human review, risk judgment, and decision ownership inside AI-assisted workflows, instead of letting automation create uncontrolled outcomes.

6. Launch Readiness

Reviewing whether a product is ready to be shared, including usability, readability, CTA clarity, link previews, mobile experience, safety notes, and delivery quality.

Current Direction

I am organizing these capabilities into a Product Builder System that can be demonstrated, delivered, and reused across web apps, AI tools, portfolio pages, content workflows, and small product prototypes.

Case Notes | PhotoCheck Docs

Productization Experiment for AI Document Reconstruction

01 · The Problem

Converting photos or PDFs into editable digital documents traditionally requires manual re-entry, reformatting tables, and restructuring layouts. This is not just an OCR problem — it is a document reconstruction and data digitization workflow problem.

02 · My Solution

Case Notes | Audio QA

Productization Experiment for AI Audio Quality Assurance

01 · The Problem

After completing AI music or voice work, creators still need to judge whether the audio quality is stable, volume appropriate, and whether there are noise, clipping, breaks, or obvious defects. Manual listening alone is prone to missing details and difficult to turn into a repeatable quality process.

02 · My Solution

Audio QA organizes the audio inspection process into an actionable QA tool — helping creators document audio status, organize issues, and establish pre-release check standards so it is easier to decide whether a piece is ready for the next step.

03 · My Role

Product positioning, user flow, AI audio QA logic, prompt engineering, test rules, interface content, and website integration.

04 · Current Status

Public demo available. Continuously optimizing audio issue classification, inspection workflow, and user experience.

Live · Continuously Improving
05 · Next Steps

Improve audio defect classification, pre-release checklists, batch inspection workflows, and AI music release pipeline integration.

Case Notes | Proof of Rights

Productization Experiment for AI Music Rights Documentation

01 · The Problem

After completing AI music work, creators cannot clearly explain their creation process, models, or prompts — making it difficult to establish credible rights documentation, creating uncertainty in release and licensing.

02 · My Solution

Proof of Rights guides creators through documenting creation records, converting them into structured rights documents exportable as PDF, JSON, or copied directly — making pre-release preparation more systematic and traceable.

03 · My Contribution

Case Notes | Distributor Fit Quiz

Productization Experiment for AI Music Distributor Selection

01 · The Problem

AI music creators face many distribution platform choices without clear criteria. Different platforms have vastly different policies on AI-generated music, Content ID rules, and royalty structures — wrong choices can cause copyright disputes or revenue loss.

02 · My Solution

7 questions assess release frequency, AI involvement, Content ID needs, and long-term cost considerations — recommending the best release path with pros and cons for each platform.

03 · My Contribution

Product strategy, quiz logic design, AI music distribution research, UX design, QA testing, and delivery.
